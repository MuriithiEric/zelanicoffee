What is Zelani Coffee?

The meaning of Zelani

The name Zelani is inspired by the birthplace of coffee, Ethiopia and means nomad/traveler or wanderer. Zelani Coffee is a Kenyan brand that is 100% pure Arabica coffee. It is our hope that this coffee wanders and finds its way not only to your home, but to your heart and soul, and discover why coffee is truly a sacred drink planted by the noble farmers of Kenya and offered to people of distinct taste and an appreciation for great-tasting coffee.
Fairchain
We are a fairchain coffee company that is fighting for the rights of farmers and cooperative societies in Kenya starting with the region that we source for our coffee, the highlands of Mt. Kenya region at its slopes at a place called Mwirua in Kirinyaga County where our founder hails from. Watch this space to see what initiatives we are undertaking and how you can be a part of this movement.

What We Do

Sourcing for Coffee

At Zelani Coffee, we believe that exceptional coffee begins with exceptional partnerships. Our journey to deliver the perfect cup starts in the heart of coffee-growing regions across Kenya

Value Addition

At Zelani Coffee, we believe in maximizing the potential of every coffee bean. Our value-added process transforms raw green beans into exceptional coffee products that delight coffee lovers worldwide.

Local & International Sales

Zelani Coffee is committed to sharing the rich flavors of our ethically sourced coffee with the world. We offer a diverse range of coffee products to cater to the preferences of coffee enthusiasts everywhere.
